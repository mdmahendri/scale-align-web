J581 Publishing of books, newspapers, periodicals and other publishing activities
This group includes activities of publishing books, newspapers, magazines and other periodicals, directories, compilations, mailing lists and other works such as catalogs, photos, calendars, greeting cards, postcards, forms, posters and reproductions of works of art.
These works are characterized by the intellectual creativity required in their development and are usually protected by copyright.
All the feasible forms of publishing (in print, electronic, digital, analog, or any other form) are included.
