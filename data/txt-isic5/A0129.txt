A0129 Growing of other perennial crops
This class includes:
- growing of rubber trees for harvesting of latex
- growing of Christmas trees
- growing of trees for extraction of sap
- growing of vegetable materials of a kind used primarily for plaiting
- growing of short rotation coppice as energy crop, e.g. poplar and willowIncludes also: .
This class excludes:
- growing of flowers, production of cut flower buds and growing of flower seeds, see 0119
- gathering of tree sap or rubber-like gums in the wild, see 0230
