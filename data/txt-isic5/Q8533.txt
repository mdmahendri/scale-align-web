Q8533 Post-secondary non-tertiary education
This class includes provision of post-secondary education, general and vocational, which cannot be considered tertiary education.
This level concerns post-secondary non-tertiary general and vocational education and training programmes.
This may also include provision of supplementary post-secondary education to prepare for tertiary education or post-secondary non-tertiary vocational.
This class excludes:
- non-formal education and training, see 855
- technical and vocational higher education, see 854
