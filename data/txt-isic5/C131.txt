C131 Spinning, weaving and finishing of textiles
This group includes the manufacture of textiles, including preparatory operations, spinning of textile fibres and weaving of textiles.
This can be done from varying raw materials, such as silk, wool, other animal, vegetable or man-made fibres, paper or glass etc.
Also included in this group is the finishing of textiles and wearing apparel, i.e. bleaching, dyeing, dressing and similar activities.
