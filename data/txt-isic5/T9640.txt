T9640 Intermediation service activities for personal services
This class includes the intermediation of personal services that bring clients and service providers together for a fee or commission, without the intermediary providing the services intermediated.
These intermediation activities can be carried out on digital platforms or through non-digital channels (face-to-face including door-to-door, phone, mail, etc.).
The fee or commission can be received from either the client or the provider of the personal service.
Revenue for the intermediation activities can include other sources of income, such as revenues from advertising.
It includes, for example, laundry and dry-cleaning agent activities.
