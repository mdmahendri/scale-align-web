Q8520 Primary education
This class includes primary education: the furnishing of academic courses and associated course work that give students a sound basic education in reading, writing and mathematics and an elementary understanding of other subjects such as history, geography, natural science, social science, art and music.
This class includes also adult literacy programmes, second chance or re-integration primary education programmes or any kind of primary education for adults.
This class excludes:
- non-formal education and training disregarding the age or field of education, see 855
- child day-care activities, including day nurseries for pupils, see 8890
