O8121 General cleaning of buildings
This class includes:
- general (non-specialised) cleaning of all types of buildings, such as:
offices
private dwellings, e.g. houses or apartments
factories
shops
institutions
- general (non-specialised) cleaning of other business and professional premises and multiunit residential buildings
These general cleaning activities are mostly interior cleaning although they may include the cleaning of associated exterior areas such as windows or passageways.
This class excludes:
- specialised cleaning activities, e.g. cleaning of windows, chimneys, fireplaces, boilers, exhaust units, see 8129
