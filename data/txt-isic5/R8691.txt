R8691 Intermediation service activities for medical, dental, and other human health services
This class includes the intermediation of medical, dental and other human health services, by bringing clients and service providers together for a fee or commission, without the intermediary providing the medical, dental and other human health services that are intermediated.
These intermediation activities can be carried out on digital platforms or through non-digital channels (face-to-face including door-to-door, phone, mail etc.).
The fee or commission can be received from either the client or the provider of the medical, dental and other human health services.
Revenue for the intermediation activities can include other sources of income, such as revenues from sale of advertising.
This class also includes:
- appointment reservations for medical visits
This class excludes:
- intermediation service activities for residential care activities, see 8791
