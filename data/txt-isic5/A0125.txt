A0125 Growing of other tree and bush fruits and nuts
This class includes:
- growing of berries such as: blueberries currants gooseberries kiwi fruit raspberries strawberries
- growing of fruit seeds
- growing of edible nuts such as: almonds cashew nuts chestnuts hazelnuts pistachios walnuts
- growing of other tree and bush fruits such as: locust beans.
This class excludes:
- growing of coconuts, see 0126
