C2396 Cutting, shaping and finishing of stone
This class includes:
- cutting, shaping and finishing of stone e.g. for use in construction, in cemeteries, on roads, as roofing
- manufacture of stone furniture
This class includes also:
- manufacture of table tops of stone
This class excludes:
- activities carried out by operators of quarries, e.g. production of rough cut stone, see 0810
- production of millstones, abrasive stones and similar products, see 239
