Q8510 Pre-primary education
This class includes:
- pre-primary education.
Pre-primary education is defined as the initial stage of organised instruction designed primarily to introduce very young children to a school- type environment, that is, to provide a bridge between the home and a school-based atmosphere.
This class excludes:
- child day-care activities (ISCED-P 2011 level 01 early childhood educational development), see 8890
