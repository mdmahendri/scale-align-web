I Accommodation and food service activities
This section includes the provision of short-term (less than one year) accommodation in buildings, camping grounds and recreational vehicle parks for visitors and other travelers and the provision of meals and drinks fit for immediate consumption.
The amount and type of supplementary services provided within this section can vary widely.
This section excludes:
- the preparation of food or drinks for resale through wholesale or retail trade activities, see section G
- the preparation of these foods is classified in manufacturing, see section C
