B08 Other mining and quarrying
This division includes extraction from a mine or quarry, but also dredging of alluvial deposits, rock
crushing and the use of salt marshes.
The products are used most notably in construction (e.g. sands,
stones), manufacture of materials (e.g. clay, gypsum, calcium) and manufacture of chemicals etc.
This division also includes the extraction of salt from sea water, brine, and other saline waters.
This division does not include manufacture of chemicals and chemical products , basic metals, non-metallic mineral products, and other further processing of extracted minerals, see section C.
