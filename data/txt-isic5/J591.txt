J591 Motion picture, video and television programme activities
This group includes the production of theatrical and non-theatrical motion pictures, videos or audiovisual works and vlogs, whether on film, DVD or other digital storage media, including digital distribution, for direct projection in theatres or for broadcasting or streaming; supporting post-production activities such as film editing, cutting, dubbing or conversion to video streaming formats ; distribution of motion pictures or other audiovisual productions to other industries as well as their projection.
Buying and selling of motion picture or other audiovisual production distribution rights are also included.
