T9540 Intermediation service activities for repair and maintenance of computers, personal and household goods, and motor vehicles and motorcycles
This class includes the intermediation of the repair and maintenance of computers, personal and household goods and motor vehicles and motorcycles by bringing clients and service providers together for a fee or commission, without the intermediary providing the services intermediated.
These intermediation activities can be carried out on digital platforms or through non-digital channels (face-to-face including door-to-door, phone, mail, etc.).
The fee or commission can be received from either the client or the provider of the repair and maintenance service.
Revenue for the intermediation activities can include other sources of income, such as revenues from advertising.
