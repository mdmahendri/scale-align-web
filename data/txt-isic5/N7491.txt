N7491 Patent brokering and marketing service activities
This class includes:
- brokering and marketing activities on patents
This class includes also:
- management services for copyrights and their revenues
- management services for rights to industrial property (patents, licences, trademarks, franchises etc.)
- activities of collective rights management organisations
This class excludes:
- leasing of intellectual property and similar products, see 7740
- buying and selling patents and similar intellectual property rights on own account, see 7740
