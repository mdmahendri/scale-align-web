J6031 News agency activities
This class includes:
- news syndicate and news agency activities furnishing news, pictures and features to the media, publishers and broadcasters
This class excludes:
- publishing activities, see 581
- broadcasting and third party on-demand streaming distribution activities , see 6010, 6020
- activities of independent photojournalists, see 7420
- activities of independent journalists, see 9011
- retail outlets of newspapers, see 4761
