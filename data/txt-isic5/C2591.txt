C2591 Forging, pressing, stamping and roll-forming of metal; powder metallurgy
This class includes:
- forging, pressing, stamping and roll-forming of metal
-production of metal objects directly from metal powders by heat treatment
(sintering) or under pressure, i.e. powder metallurgy
This class also includes:
- subsequent trimming or de-scaling of the metal, without any further processing
This class excludes:
- production of metal powder, see 241, 242
