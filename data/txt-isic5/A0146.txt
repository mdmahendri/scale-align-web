A0146 Raising of poultry
This class includes:
- raising and breeding of poultry: chickens, turkeys, ducks, geese, quails and guinea fowls
- production of eggs from poultry
- operation of poultry hatcheries .
This class includes also:
- raising and breeding of quails
- production of quail eggs.
This class excludes:
- production of feathers or down, see 1010
