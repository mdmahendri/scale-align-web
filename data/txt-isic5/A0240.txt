A0240 Support services to forestry
This class includes carrying out part of the forestry operation on a fee or contract basis.
This class includes:
- forestry service activities: forestry inventories forest management consulting services timber evaluation forest fire prevention forest pest control
- logging service activities: transport of logs within the forest.
This class excludes:
- operation of forest tree nurseries, see 0210
- draining of forestry land, see 4312
- clearing of building sites, see 4312
