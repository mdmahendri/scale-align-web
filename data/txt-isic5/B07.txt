B07 Mining of metal ores
This division includes mining for metallic minerals (ores), performed through underground or open- cast extraction, seabed mining etc.
This division also includes:
- ore dressing and beneficiating operations, such as crushing, grinding, washing, drying, sintering, calcining or leaching ore, gravity separation or flotation operations.
This division excludes:
- roasting of iron pyrites, see 2011
- production of aluminium oxide, see 2420
- operation of blast furnaces, see division 24
