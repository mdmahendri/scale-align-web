Q8559 Other education n.e.c.
This class includes:
- education that is not definable by level

- remedial courses
- professional examination review courses

- computer training
- religious instruction
- wellness courses,
- sewing courses,
- relaxation courses,
- oenology courses,
- family life courses, e.g. parent-child courses
