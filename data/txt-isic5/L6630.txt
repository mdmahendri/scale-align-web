L6630 Fund management activities
This class includes portfolio and fund management activities, for instance making decisions on which investments to purchase or sell, on a fee or contract basis, for individuals, businesses and others, e.g.:
- management of mutual funds
- management of other investment funds
- management of pension funds
This class includes also
- activities of asset management, on a fee or contract basis
This class excludes
- dealing in financial markets on own-account, see 6499
- management of real estate, see division 68
