C1074 Manufacture of macaroni, noodles, couscous and similar farinaceous products
This class includes:
- manufacture of pastas such as macaroni and noodles, whether or not cooked or stuffed
- manufacture of semolina (couscous).
This class excludes:
- manufacture of prepared meals and dishes based on pasta and of couscous dishes, see 1075
- manufacture of soup containing pasta, see 1079
