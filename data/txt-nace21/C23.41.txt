C23.41 Manufacture of ceramic household and ornamental articles
This class includes:
- manufacture of ceramic tableware, kitchenware, household articles, toilet articles, statuettes and other ornamental articles of ceramics (e.g. porcelain, china, common pottery, stoneware, earthenware or fine pottery)
This class excludes:
- manufacture of imitation jewellery, see 32.13
- manufacture of ceramic toys, see 32.40
