B07 Mining of metal ores
This division includes mining for metallic minerals (ores), performed through underground or open-cast extraction, seabed mining, and so on.
This division also includes:
- ore dressing and beneficiating operations (e.g. crushing, grinding, washing, drying, sintering, calcining or leaching ore, gravity separation or flotation operations)
This division excludes:
- roasting of iron pyrites, see 20.13
- operation of blast furnaces, see division 24
- production of aluminium oxide, see 24.42
