G47.81 Retail sale of motor vehicles
This class includes:
- retail sale of new and used motor vehicles, including electric vehicles:
passenger motor vehicles
camping vehicles (e.g. caravans, motorhomes)
This class also includes:
- retail sale of off-road motor vehicles
- retail sale of trailers for motor vehicles
This class excludes:
- retail sale of bicycles and electric bicycles etc., see 47.63
- retail sale of parts and accessories for motor vehicles, see 47.82
- retail sale of motorcycles, motorcycle parts and accessories, see 47.83
- activities of agents for the retail sale of motor vehicles, see 47.9
- on-demand passenger transport service activities by vehicle with a driver, see 49.33
- rental and leasing of motor vehicles without a driver, see 77.1
- maintenance and repair of motor vehicles and motorcycles, see 95.3
