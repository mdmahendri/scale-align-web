R86.94 Nursing and midwifery activities
This class includes nursing and midwifery activities not performed by medical doctors, for example:
- activities of independent nurses, who work in own consulting rooms or at patients homes
- activities of maternity facilities, without permanent supervision of medical doctors
This class also includes:
- palliative nursing care activities for outpatients
This class excludes:
- maternity wards in hospitals, under the direct supervision of medical doctors, see 86.10
- residential nursing care facilities, see 87.10
- homecare services, see 88.10
