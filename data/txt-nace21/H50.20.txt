H50.20 Sea and coastal freight water transport
This class includes:
- transport of freight over sea and coastal waters
- transport by towing or pushing of barges, oil rigs, etc.
This class also includes:
- rental of vessels with crew for sea and coastal freight water transport
- ferry transport of unaccompanied vehicles
This class excludes:
- ferry transport of passengers with or without a vehicle, see 50.10
- storage of freight, see 52.10
- harbour operations and other auxiliary activities (e.g. docking, pilotage, lighterage, vessel salvage), see 52.22
- cargo handling, see 52.24
- rental of commercial ships or boats without crew, see 77.34
