C23.32 Manufacture of bricks, tiles and construction products, in baked clay
This class includes:
- manufacture of structural non-refractory ceramic articles, obtained by firing clay building materials (e.g. ceramic bricks, roofing tiles, chimney pots, pipes, conduits)
- manufacture of flooring blocks in baked clay
This class excludes:
- manufacture of refractory ceramic products, see 23.20
- manufacture of non-structural non-refractory ceramic products, see 23.4
