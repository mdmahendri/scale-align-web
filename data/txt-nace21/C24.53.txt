C24.53 Casting of light metals
This class includes:
- activities of foundries of light metal
- manufacture of castings made of aluminium
- manufacture of castings made of magnesium
- manufacture of castings made of titanium
This class also includes:
- casting of dental implements, etc., of light metals
This class excludes:
- casting of metals not considered light metals, see 24.51, 24.52, 24.54
