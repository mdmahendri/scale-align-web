B07.29 Mining of other non-ferrous metal ores
This class includes:
- mining and preparation of ores chiefly valued for non-ferrous metal content:
aluminium (bauxite), copper, lead, zinc, tin, manganese, chrome, nickel, cobalt, molybdenum, tantalum, vanadium, lithium/spodumene, etc.
precious metals: gold, silver, platinum
This class excludes:
- mining and preparation of uranium and thorium ores, see 07.21
- production of aluminium oxide, see 24.42
- production of mattes of copper, see 24.44
- production of mattes of nickel, see 24.45
