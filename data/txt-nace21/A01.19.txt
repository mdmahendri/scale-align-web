A01.19 Growing of other non-perennial crops
This class includes the growing of all other non-perennial crops, e.g.:
- growing of clover, alfalfa, sainfoin, maize, lupines, other grasses, fodder kale and similar forage crops
- growing of beet seeds and seeds of forage plants
- growing of flowers
- cutting of flowers and flower buds
- growing of flower seeds
This class also includes:
- growing of perennial flowers and other ornamental plants
This class excludes:
- growing of sugar beet seeds, see 01.13
- growing of non-perennial spices and aromatic, drug and pharmaceutical crops, see 01.28
