G46.32 Wholesale of meat, meat products, fish and fish products
This class includes:
- wholesale of meat and meat products
- wholesale of poultry and game
- wholesale of fish and fish products
- wholesale of seafood
This class also includes:
- wholesale of tripe, delicatessen (charcuterie), salted meat
- wholesale of snails
- wholesale of algae and seaweed
This class excludes:
- wholesale of livestock feed, see 46.21
- wholesale of ready-made meals, see 46.38
- wholesale of food for pets, see 46.38
