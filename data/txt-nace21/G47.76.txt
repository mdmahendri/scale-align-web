G47.76 Retail sale of flowers, plants, fertilisers, pets and pet food
This class includes:
- retail sale of flowers and plants
- retail sale of fertilisers and plant protection products
- retail sale of pets and articles for pets
- retail sale of food for pets and other animals kept at home for pleasure (e.g. chickens, horses)
This class also includes:
- retail sale of toiletries, hygiene products, etc. for pets
