C15.11 Tanning, dressing, dyeing of leather and fur
This class includes:
- tanning, dyeing and dressing of hides, skins and pre-tanned leather
- manufacture of chamois dressed, parchment dressed, patent or metallised leathers
- manufacture of composition leather
- scraping, shearing, plucking, currying, tanning, bleaching and dyeing of fur skins and hides with the hair on
This class excludes:
- production of hides and skins as part of ranching, see 01.4
- production of raw hides and skins as part of slaughtering, see 10.11
- manufacture of leather apparel, see 14.24
- manufacture of imitation leather not based on natural leather, see 22.12, 22.26
