T95.22 Repair and maintenance of household appliances and home and garden equipment
This class includes the repair and servicing of household appliances and home and garden equipment:
- repair and servicing of household appliances:
refrigerators, stoves, washing machines, clothes/tumble dryers, room air conditioners, etc.
- repair and servicing of home and garden equipment:
lawnmowers, edgers, snowblowers, leaf blowers, trimmers, etc.
This class excludes:
- repair of handheld power tools and commercial refrigeration, see 33.12
- repair of central air-conditioning systems, see 43.22
