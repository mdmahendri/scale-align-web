C24.45 Other non-ferrous metal production
This class includes:
- production of unwrought chrome, manganese, nickel, etc., alloyed or not alloyed, from ores or oxides or from electrolytic and aluminothermic refining of chrome, manganese, nickel, etc., waste and scrap
- manufacture of bars, rods, profiles and wire of chrome, manganese, nickel, etc.
- manufacture of plates, sheets, strip and foil of chrome, manganese, nickel, etc.
- production of mattes of nickel
- production of other non-ferrous metals
This class excludes:
- casting of non-ferrous metals, see 24.53, 24.54
- shredding of non-ferrous metal waste and scrap, see 38.21
