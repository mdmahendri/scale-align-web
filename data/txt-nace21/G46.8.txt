G46.8 Other specialised wholesale
This group includes:
- other specialised wholesale activities not classified in groups 46.2 to 46.7 of this division.
- the wholesale of intermediate products, except agricultural, typically not for household use
This group excludes:
- activities of wholesalers who do not assume ownership of the goods they trade, see 46.1
- wholesale of batteries and accumulators not for motor vehicles, see 46.64
- non-specialised wholesale trade, see 46.90
