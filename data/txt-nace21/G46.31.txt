G46.31 Wholesale of fruit and vegetables
This class includes:
- wholesale of fresh fruits and vegetables
- wholesale of legumes
- wholesale of dried fruits and vegetables
- wholesale of preserved fruits and vegetables
- wholesale of frozen fruits and vegetables
This class also includes:
- wholesale of mushrooms
This class excludes:
- wholesale of fruit and vegetable juices, see 46.34
- wholesale of ready-made meals, see 46.38
