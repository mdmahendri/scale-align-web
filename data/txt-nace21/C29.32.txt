C29.32 Manufacture of other parts and accessories for motor vehicles
This class includes:
- manufacture of diverse parts and accessories for motor vehicles, e.g.:
gearboxes, clutches, brakes, axles, road wheels, shock absorbers
- manufacture of parts and accessories of bodies for motor vehicles, e.g.:
bumpers, safety seat belts, safety airbags, rear-view mirrors
- manufacture of car seats, including seats for babies and children
- manufacture of air-conditioning machines for motor vehicles
This class excludes:
- manufacture of tyres, see 22.11
- manufacture of rubber hoses and belts, and other rubber products, see 22.12
- manufacture of pistons, piston rings and carburettors not for motor vehicles or motorcycles, see 28.11
- manufacture of pumps for motor vehicles and engines, see 28.13
- repair, maintenance and alteration of motor vehicles, see 95.31
