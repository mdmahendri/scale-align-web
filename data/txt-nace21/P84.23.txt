P84.23 Justice and judicial activities
This class includes:
- administration and operation of administrative, civil and criminal law courts, military tribunals, and the judicial system
- rendering of judgements and interpretations of the law
- activities of public prosecutors
- arbitration of civil actions
- prison administration and the provision of prison services, including rehabilitation services
This class excludes:
- legal advice and representation in civil, criminal and other cases, see 69.10
- activities of prison schools, see division 85
- activities of prison hospitals, see 86.10
