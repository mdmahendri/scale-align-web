G47.71 Retail sale of clothing
This class includes:
- retail sale of articles of clothing, including underwear
- retail sale of fur articles
- retail sale of clothing accessories, (e.g. gloves, ties, braces)
This class also includes:
- retail sale of work clothes, uniforms, disguises, carnival costumes, etc.
This class excludes:
- retail sale of textiles, see 47.51
- retail sale of special sports equipment clothes for skiing, martial arts, ballet, etc., see 47.63
- retail sale of protective gear for cyclists, see 47.63
- retail sale of second-hand clothing, see 47.79
- retail sale of protective gear for motorcyclists, see 47.83
