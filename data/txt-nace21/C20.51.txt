C20.51 Manufacture of liquid biofuels
This class includes:
- manufacture of biodiesel
- manufacture of ethanol from biomass
- manufacture of mixtures of bioethanol and ethers partially derived from biomass
- manufacture of liquid biofuels even if processes use waste as an input
This class excludes:
- manufacture of solid fuels from vegetable biomass, see 16.26
- manufacture of ethyl alcohol other than for bioethanol biofuels, see 20.14
- manufacture of explosives, see 20.59
