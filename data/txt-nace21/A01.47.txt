A01.47 Raising of poultry
This class includes:
- raising and breeding of poultry, e.g.:
chickens, turkeys, ducks, geese, quails, ostriches or guineafowls
- production of eggs from poultry
- operation of poultry hatcheries
- automated egg hatching for poultry
This class excludes:
- production of feathers or down, see 10.12
