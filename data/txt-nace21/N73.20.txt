N73.20 Market research and public opinion polling
This class includes:
- conducting market research studies on the awareness, evaluation and use of goods and services, on product development and pricing, on the use of media and on the recall and impact of advertising
- conducting studies of opinion research on the quality of life and lifestyles, on social participation and political participation, on political party preference and voting behaviour
This class also includes scientific conducting of market and opinion research with:
- qualitative and quantitative methodological orientation
- reactive and non-reactive survey procedures
- collection of research data through questioning, observation, measurement and counting
- face-to-face, postal, telephone and online interviews to collect research data
This class excludes:
- advertising, see 73.1
- public relations and communication activities, see 73.30
- undertaking telephone interviews for market and opinion research surveys on a contract or fee basis, see 82.20
