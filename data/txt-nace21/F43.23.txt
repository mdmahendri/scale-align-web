F43.23 Installation of insulation
This class includes:
- thermal insulation works (e.g. for pipes, boilers and ducts, interior or exterior, including cladding for insulation)
- sound insulation works
- vibration insulation works
- fireproofing works
- hydro insulation
- internal insulation of roofs
This class excludes:
- external insulation of roofs, see 43.41
- waterproofing of roofs, see 43.41
