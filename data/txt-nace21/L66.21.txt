L66.21 Risk and damage evaluation
This class includes the provision of administration services of insurance, for example:
- assessing insurance claims
claims adjusting
risk assessing
risk and damage evaluation
average and loss adjusting
- settling insurance claims
This class excludes:
- appraisal of real estate, see 68.31
- appraisal for other purposes, see 74.99
- investigation activities, see 80.01
