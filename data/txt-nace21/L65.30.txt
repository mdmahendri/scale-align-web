L65.30 Pension funding
This class includes the provision of retirement income benefits exclusively for the sponsors employees or members and other insurance products regulated as employer-related funds.
It includes pension plans with defined benefits as well as defined contributions.
This class includes:
- provision of employee benefit plans
- provision of pension funds and plans
- provision of retirement plans
This class excludes:
- management of pension funds, see 66.30
- compulsory social security schemes, see 84.30
