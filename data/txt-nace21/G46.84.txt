G46.84 Wholesale of hardware, plumbing and heating equipment and supplies
This class includes:
- wholesale of ironware and similar household hardware
- wholesale of fittings
- wholesale of hot water heaters, radiators and boilers
- wholesale of sanitary installation equipment:
tubes, pipes, fittings, taps, T pieces, connections, rubber pipes, etc.
- wholesale of tools (e.g. hammers, saws, screwdrivers and other hand tools), including power hand tools
- wholesale of heating, ventilation and air-conditioning equipment, for non-domestic use
This class excludes:
- wholesale of heating, ventilation and air-conditioning equipment, for domestic use, see 46.43
- wholesale of mining, construction and civil engineering machinery, see 46.63
- wholesale of sanitary equipment, see 46.83
