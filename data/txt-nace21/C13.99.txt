C13.99 Manufacture of other textiles n.e.c.
This class includes:
- manufacture of felt
- manufacture of tulles and other net fabrics, of lace and embroidery, in the piece, in strips or in motifs
- manufacture of pressure-sensitive cloth-tape
- manufacture of shoelaces, of textiles
- manufacture of net and window furnishing type fabrics of lace
This class excludes:
- manufacture of needle-loom felt floor coverings, see 13.93
- manufacture of textile wadding and articles of wadding: sanitary towels, tampons, etc., see 17.22
