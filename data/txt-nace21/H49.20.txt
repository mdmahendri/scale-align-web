H49.20 Freight rail transport
This class includes:
- freight transport on mainline rail networks as well as short line freight railways
This class also includes:
- freight transport by funicular railway
- traction services
This class excludes:
- warehousing and storage, see 52.10
- freight terminal activities, see 52.21
- operation of railway infrastructure and related activities (e.g. switching and shunting), see 52.21
- cargo handling on behalf of others, see 52.24
