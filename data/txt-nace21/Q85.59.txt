Q85.59 Other education n.e.c.
This class includes:
- education that is not definable by level
- academic tutoring
- remedial courses
- professional examination review courses
- language instruction and conversational skills instruction
- computer training (e.g. general computer use, programming courses, use of computer applications)
- relaxation courses (e.g. meditation courses, breathing techniques, courses of awareness through movement, courses on stress and anxiety relief techniques)
- sewing courses
- oenology courses
- family life courses (e.g. parentchild courses)
- training on plant protection products
This class also includes:
- lifeguard training
- survival training
- public speaking training
- speed-reading instruction
- activities of professional instructors and teachers in subjects other than sports, arts and driving
This class excludes:
- formal adult literacy programmes, see 85.20
- general secondary education, see 85.31
- technical and vocational secondary education and training, see 85.32
- entertainment activities in retirement homes, see 93.29
