A01.25 Growing of other tree and bush fruits and nuts
This class includes:
- growing of berries, e.g.:
blueberries
fresh currants
gooseberries
kiwi fruit
raspberries
strawberries
sea buckthorn
- growing of fruit seeds
- growing of edible nuts, e.g.:
almonds
cashew nuts
chestnuts
hazelnuts
pistachios
walnuts
pine nuts
- growing of other tree and bush fruits, e.g.:
locust beans
This class excludes:
- growing of coconuts, see 01.26
