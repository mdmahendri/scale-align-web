C10.6 Manufacture of grain mill products, starches and starch products
This group includes the milling of flour or meal from grains or vegetables, the cleaning, polishing and milling of rice, as well as the micronisation and thermal processing of flour and the manufacture of flour mixes or doughs from these products.
Also included in this group are the wet milling of corn and vegetables and the manufacture of starch and starch products.
This group also includes the manufacture of gluten-free mill products.
This group excludes:
- grain drying on a fee or contract basis, see 01.63
