G46.72 Wholesale of motor vehicle parts and accessories
This class includes:
- wholesale of new and used motor vehicle parts, equipment and related accessories like tyres, etc.
- wholesale of batteries and accumulators for motor vehicles
This class excludes:
- activities of agents involved in the wholesale of cars and light motor vehicles parts and accessories, see 46.18
- wholesale of boats, planes, trains, including parts and accessories, see 46.64
