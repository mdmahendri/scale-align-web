B08.92 Extraction of peat
This class includes:
- peat digging
- preparation of peat to improve quality or facilitate transport/storage
This class excludes:
- service activities incidental to peat mining, see 09.90
- manufacture of peat briquettes, see 19.20
- manufacture of potting soil mixtures (e.g. of peat, natural soil, sands, clays, fertiliser minerals), see 20.15
- manufacture of articles of peat, see 23.99
