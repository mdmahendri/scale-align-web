G46.43 Wholesale of electrical household appliances
This class includes:
- wholesale of electrical household appliances
- wholesale of radio and television equipment
- wholesale of photographic and optical goods except corrective glasses, lenses and sunglasses
- wholesale of electrical heating and cooling appliances
- wholesale of heating equipment using renewable resources
- wholesale of heating, ventilation and air-conditioning equipment
- wholesale of sewing machines of household-type
- wholesale of safety systems and alarms
This class excludes:
- wholesale of corrective glasses, lenses and sunglasses, see 46.46
- wholesale of recorded media, see 46.49
- wholesale of recordable media, see 46.50
- wholesale of sewing machines other than household-type, see 46.64
- wholesale of hardware, plumbing and heating equipment and supplies, see 46.84
- wholesale of heating, ventilation and air-conditioning equipment for non-domestic use, see 46.84
