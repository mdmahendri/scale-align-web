C26.12 Manufacture of loaded electronic boards
This class includes:
- manufacture of loaded printed circuit boards consisting of conductor elements, contacts and other passive elements
- loading of passive elements (components) onto printed circuit boards
- manufacture of interface cards (e.g. sound, video, controllers, network, modems) loaded with passive elements
This class excludes:
- printing of smart cards, see 18.12
- manufacture of bare printed circuit boards, consisting only of conductor elements and contacts, see 26.11
