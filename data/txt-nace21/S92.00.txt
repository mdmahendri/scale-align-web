S92.00 Gambling and betting activities
This class includes gambling and betting activities, for example:
- bookmaking and other betting operations
- off-track betting
- operation of casinos (including floating casinos)
- sale of lottery tickets
- operation (exploitation) of coin-operated gambling machines
- quiz app subscriptions with prizes
- sports betting
- operation of card games as a betting activity
- operation of gambling or lottery games
This class excludes:
- operation of clubs for card games (e.g. bridge clubs), see 93.12
