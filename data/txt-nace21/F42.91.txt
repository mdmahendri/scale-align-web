F42.91 Construction of water projects
This class includes:
- construction and reconstruction of, e.g.:
waterways, harbour basins, rivers, marinas
hydromechanical structures (e.g. locks, lifts, sluices, slipways)
dams and dykes
- dredging of waterways (e.g. harbour basins, rivers)
This class excludes:
- construction of underwater pipelines, see 42.21
- laying of underwater cables, see 42.22
- project management activities related to civil engineering works, see 71.12
