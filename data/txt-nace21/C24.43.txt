C24.43 Lead, zinc and tin production
This class includes:
- production of unwrought lead, zinc and tin, alloyed or not alloyed, from ores or from lead, zinc or tin waste and scrap
- manufacture of bars, rods, profiles, wire, plates, sheets, strip and foil of lead, zinc or tin
This class also includes:
- manufacture of zinc powder by atomisation of molten zinc
This class excludes:
- casting of lead, zinc or tin, see 24.54
- shredding of lead, zinc or tin waste and scrap, see 38.21
