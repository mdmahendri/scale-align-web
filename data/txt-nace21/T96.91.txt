T96.91 Provision of domestic personal service activities
This class includes the provision of a range of domestic personal service activities like cooking, washing, cleaning and ironing services, at home for households.
When only one activity is performed it is classified according to this activity.
This class excludes:
- cleaning of buildings, see 81.21
- home care services for older persons, including live-in care services, see 88.10
- day-care activities for children provided in private homes or day-care centres, see 88.91
- after-school care services, see 88.91
