C10.71 Manufacture of bread; manufacture of fresh pastry goods and cakes
This class includes:
- manufacture of bakery products:
bread and rolls
pastry, cakes, pies, tarts, pancakes, waffles, etc.
This class also includes:
- manufacture of pre-baked bread
This class excludes:
- manufacture of dry bakery products, see 10.72
- manufacture of pastas, see 10.73
- retail sale of bread and rolls baked on the premises from pre-baked products, see 47.24
