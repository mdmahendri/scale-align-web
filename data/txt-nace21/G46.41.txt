G46.41 Wholesale of textiles
This class includes:
- wholesale of yarn
- wholesale of fabrics
- wholesale of household linen, etc.
- wholesale of haberdashery: needles, sewing thread, etc.
- wholesale of parasols, tarpaulins, sails, parachutes, etc.
This class excludes:
- wholesale of clothing, see 46.42
- wholesale of tents and sleeping bags, see 46.49
- wholesale of textile fibres, see 46.86
