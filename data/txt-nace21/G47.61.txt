G47.61 Retail sale of books
This class includes:
- retail sale of books, including audiobooks on physical media
This class excludes:
- retail sale of recorded media except audiobooks on physical media, see 47.69
- retail sale of antique books, see 47.79
- retail sale of second-hand books, see 47.79
- distributing e-books and audiobooks via streaming and downloading by book publishers, see 58.11
