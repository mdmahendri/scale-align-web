C23.66 Manufacture of other articles of concrete, cement and plaster
This class includes:
- manufacture of other articles of concrete, plaster or of compositions based on plaster, cement or artificial stone (e.g. statuary, bas- and haut-reliefs, vases, flowerpots)
This class also includes:
- manufacture of ornamented boards or panels of plaster or of compositions based on plaster
- other articles of calcium silicate
- other articles of autoclaved aerated concrete
This class excludes:
- manufacture of precast concrete, cement or artificial stone articles for use in construction, see 23.61
