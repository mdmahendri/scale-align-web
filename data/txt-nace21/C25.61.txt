C25.61 Manufacture of cutlery
This class includes:
- manufacture of domestic cutlery (e.g. knives, forks, spoons)
- manufacture of other articles of cutlery, e.g.:
cleavers and choppers
razors and razor blades
scissors and hair clippers
- manufacture of cutlasses, swords, bayonets, etc.
This class excludes:
- manufacture of knives and cutting blades for industrial machines, see 25.63
- manufacture of hollowware (pots, kettles, etc.), dinnerware (bowls, platters, etc.), or flatware (plates, saucers, etc.), see 25.99
- manufacture of cutlery of precious metal, see 32.12
