F43.42 Other specialised construction activities in construction of buildings
This class includes:
- construction activities specialising in one aspect common to construction of buildings, requiring specialised skills or equipment:
construction of foundations for buildings, including piledriving
de-humidification of buildings
erection of prefabricated, not self-manufactured, structural steel components for buildings
erection of chimneys and industrial ovens
This class excludes:
- construction or installation of masonry heater stoves, see 43.22
