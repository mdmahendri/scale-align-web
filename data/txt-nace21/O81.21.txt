O81.21 General cleaning of buildings
This class includes:
- general (non-specialised) cleaning of all types of buildings, e.g.:
offices
private dwellings (e.g. houses or apartments)
factories
shops
institutions
- general (non-specialised) cleaning of other business and professional premises and multi-unit residential buildings
These general cleaning activities are mostly interior cleaning although they may include the cleaning of associated exterior areas (for example, windows, passageways).
This class excludes:
- specialised cleaning activities (e.g. cleaning of windows, chimneys, fireplaces, boilers, exhaust units), see 81.22
