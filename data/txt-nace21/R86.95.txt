R86.95 Physiotherapy activities
This class includes therapies provided by independent professionals to improve mobility, restore function or reduce pain.
The therapies provided include physiotherapy, and so on.
This class also includes:
- activities of medical massage therapists
- activities of ergotherapists
- activities of occupational therapists
This class excludes:
- activities of osteopaths, see 86.96
- activities of chiropractors, see 86.96
- body treatment techniques (e.g. shiatsu, Thai massage, Watsu, tui na qigong), see 86.99
- activities of massage salons for non-medical massage, see 96.23
