C10.72 Manufacture of rusks, biscuits, preserved pastries and cakes
This class includes:
- manufacture of rusks, biscuits and other dry bakery products
- manufacture of preserved pastry goods, cakes and waffles
- manufacture of snack products (cookies, crackers, pretzels, etc.), whether sweet or salted
This class also includes:
- manufacture of breadcrumbs, obtained by grinding toasted bread
This class excludes:
- manufacture of potato snacks, see 10.31
