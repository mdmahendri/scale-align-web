H49.34 Passenger transport by cableways and ski lifts
This class includes:
- transport by cableways, ski lifts and chairlifts, which may include the preparation of ski slopes
- passenger transport by lifts
This class also includes:
- freight transport by cableways
This class excludes:
- passenger rail transport for sightseeing, see 49.1
- passenger transport by funicular railway, see 49.12
- non-scheduled passenger road transport in vehicles of 10 or more persons, see 49.32
- operation of sports areas, which may include the operation of cableways, ski lifts and chairlifts, see 93.11
