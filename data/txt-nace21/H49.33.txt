H49.33 On-demand passenger transport service activities by vehicle with driver
This class includes the on-demand transport of passengers in vehicles with a driver, for a maximum of 9 persons:
- taxi services
- private hire vehicles with a driver
- motorcycle taxi services
This class excludes:
- bicycle taxi services, see 49.39
- passenger road transport by vehicles drawn by humans or animals, see 49.39
- intermediation service activities for passenger transport, see 52.32
- patient transport by ambulance, see 86.92
