H49.12 Other passenger rail transport
This class includes:
- passenger transport by metro, subway, underground and elevated railway, metropolitan railway and tramway
- passenger transport by funicular railway
This class also includes:
- passenger light rail transport for sightseeing
This class excludes:
- passenger terminal activities, see 52.21
- operation of railway infrastructure and related activities (e.g. switching and shunting), see 52.21
