A02.30 Gathering of wild growing non-wood products
This class includes:
- gathering of wild growing materials, e.g.:
mushrooms, truffles
berries
nuts
balata and other rubber-like gums
cork
lac and resins
balsams
acorns, horse chestnuts
mosses and lichens
vegetable hair / eelgrass
This class also includes:
- harvesting wild herbs and associated natural drying
This class excludes:
- non-wild growing of any of these products (except growing of cork trees), see division 01
- growing of mushrooms or truffles, see 01.13
- growing of berries or nuts, see 01.25
- gathering of firewood, see 02.20
