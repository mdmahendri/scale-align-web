J60.31 News agency activities
This class includes:
- news syndicate and news agency activities furnishing news, pictures and features to the media, publishers and broadcasters
This class excludes:
- retail sale of newspapers, see 47.62
- publishing activities, see 58.1
- broadcasting and audio or video streaming activities (having distribution rights), see 60.10, 60.20
- provision of technical infrastructure related to audio or video streaming services, data processing services and related activities, see 63.10
- activities of independent photojournalists, see 74.20
- activities of independent journalists, see 90.11
