G46.34 Wholesale of beverages
This class includes:
- wholesale of alcoholic beverages
- wholesale of non-alcoholic beverages
- wholesale of fruit and vegetable juices
- wholesale of prepared beverages in cans or bottles
This class also includes:
- buying of wine in bulk and bottling without transformation
This class excludes:
- blending of wine, distilled spirits and other beverages (in order to make a new product), see 11.01, 11.02, 11.07
