H53.20 Other postal and courier activities
This class includes:
- pickup, sorting, transport and delivery (domestic (national) or international) of letter post and (mail-type) parcels and packages by businesses operating outside the scope of a universal service obligation.
One or more modes of transport may be involved and the activity may be carried out with either self-owned (private) transport or via public transport.
This class also includes:
- home delivery services, including food
This class excludes:
- transport of freight, see (according to mode of transport) 49.20, 49.41, 50.20, 50.40, 51.21, 51.22
- delivery of furniture, can include unpacking or installation, see 49.41
- home delivery of prepared meals, if performed by the same unit which prepares the meals, see division 56
