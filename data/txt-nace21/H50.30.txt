H50.30 Inland passenger water transport
This class includes:
- transport of passengers via rivers, canals, lakes and other inland waterways, including inside harbours and ports
This class also includes:
- rental of pleasure boats with crew for inland water transport
- operation of sightseeing boats and excursions on inland waterways
- inland ferry transport of passengers with or without a vehicle
This class excludes:
- restaurant and bar activities aboard ships, when provided by separate units, see 56.11, 56.30
- rental of pleasure boats and yachts without crew, see 77.21
- operation of floating casinos, not transporting passengers, see 92.00
