C28.13 Manufacture of other pumps and compressors
This class includes:
- manufacture of air or vacuum pumps, air or other gas compressors
- manufacture of pumps for liquids, whether or not fitted with a measuring device
- manufacture of pumps for internal combustion engines (e.g. oil, water and fuel pumps for motor vehicles)
This class also includes:
- manufacture of hand pumps
This class excludes:
- manufacture of hydraulic fluid power pumps, see 28.12
