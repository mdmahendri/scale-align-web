F42.13 Construction of bridges and tunnels
This class includes:
- construction of bridges (e.g. for elevated motorways and railways)
- construction of tunnels, including those for motorways and railways
This class excludes:
- construction of railways, see 42.12
- installation of lighting and electrical signals, see 43.21
- project management for construction, see 71.1
- engineering activities, see 71.12
