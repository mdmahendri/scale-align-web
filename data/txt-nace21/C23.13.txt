C23.13 Manufacture of hollow glass
This class includes:
- manufacture of bottles and other containers of glass or crystal
- manufacture of drinking glasses, glassware for table or kitchen purposes and other domestic glass or crystal articles
This class also includes:
- manufacture of glass inners for vacuum vessels
- cutting or otherwise decorating drinking glasses
This class excludes:
- printing onto glass, see 18.12
- manufacture of glass toys, see 32.40
