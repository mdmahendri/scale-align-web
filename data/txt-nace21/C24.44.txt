C24.44 Copper production
This class includes:
- manufacture of unrefined or refined unwrought copper, alloyed or not alloyed, from ores or from copper waste and scrap
- manufacture of bars, rods, profiles, plates, sheets and strip of copper and copper alloys
- manufacture of wire of copper and copper alloys
- manufacture of tubes, pipes, tube and pipe fittings of copper and copper alloys
- manufacture of copper foil, whether or not printed or backed with paper, paperboard, plastics or similar backing materials
This class also includes:
- manufacture of bars, rods, profiles, plates, sheets, strip, wire, tubes and pipes of brass
This class excludes:
- casting of copper, see 24.54
- shredding of copper waste and scrap, see 38.21
