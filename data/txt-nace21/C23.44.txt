C23.44 Manufacture of other technical ceramic products
This class includes:
- manufacture of permanent magnets and articles intended to become permanent magnets after magnetisation, of materials other than metal (e.g. agglomerated ferrite)
- manufacture of ceramic wares for laboratory, chemical or other technical uses
This class excludes:
- manufacture of refractory ceramic goods, see 23.20
- manufacture of ceramic building materials, see 23.3
- manufacture of artificial stone (e.g. cultured marble), see 23.66
